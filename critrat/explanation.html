After a year of using ChatGPT, this is the best prompt sequence that I've found. It's helped me diagnose a lingering medical issue, brainstorm marketing ideas, problem-solve interpersonal conflicts, and is now helping my friend negotiate a boat purchase. Me: [LONG explanation of a problem with way too much detail]. What type of expert would be best to help me with this issue? Chat: [expert type] Me: What questions would [expert type] want to ask to correctly diagnose this issue? Please ask me those questions one at a time. Chat: Question 1 Me: Answer 1 Chat: Question 2 Me: Answer 2 (Mostly it asks about 10 questions) Me: Based on all my answers, what would [expert type] suggest?
